Main Features

Here are some of the key features of IdeaHaven that empower creativity and connection among users:

User Registration and Authentication

Allow users to sign up for an account with a username, email, and password.

Profile Creation and Customization

Enable users to create a profile with a bio, profile picture, and other optional details.

Posting and Sharing Content

Allow users to create and publish short posts or "ideas" containing text or links.

Following and Followers

Enable users to follow other users to see their posts in their feed.

Engagement Features

Allow users to like, comment on, and share posts from other users.

Search and Explore

Implement a search feature to allow users to find specific posts, users, or topics of interest.

Messaging and Notifications

Enable users to send private messages to each other and implement notifications for new messages, mentions, and activity on posts.

Content Moderation and Reporting

Allow users to report inappropriate or abusive content and take appropriate actions.

Analytics and Insights

Provide users with insights into their post performance, such as views, likes, and comments.
